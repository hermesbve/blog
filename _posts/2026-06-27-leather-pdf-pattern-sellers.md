### Highlights

- **Best for free patterns**: [MakeSupply](https://projects.makesupply.co) — every pattern on the site is 100% free, no sign-up required. [Elktracks Studio](https://elktracksstudio.com/collections/free-patterns) also offers over 150 free tooling and carving patterns.
- **Best for variety**: [Tony See Patterns](https://www.leather-patterns.com) and [Creative Awl Studio](https://creativeawl.com) offer huge catalogs of bags, wallets, and accessories with video tutorials.
- **Best for video instruction**: [Vasile and Pavel](https://vasileandpavel.com) pair every pattern with a step-by-step video guide.
- **Community pattern library**: [LeatherLibrary.net](https://leatherlibrary.net) hosts 400+ patterns from multiple designers, filterable by difficulty.
- **Etsy-only sellers**: [DSLeatherGoods](https://www.etsy.com/shop/DSLeatherGoods), [PlaneLeatherPatterns](https://www.etsy.com/shop/PlaneLeatherPatterns), and [Teran Atelier](https://www.etsy.com/shop/TeranAtelierPatterns) sell exclusively through Etsy with strong reputations.
